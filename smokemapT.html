Terrorist Smokes




A: Between Squeaky and Site
Pros- Obscures vision for CTs behind quad and in the corner
Cons- CT's can hide behind this smoke for easy backstabs if unchecked
B: A Main
Pros- Obscures CTs vision into A main
Cons- Lose all sight of the bomb site
C: Between Redbox and Forklift
Pros- Obscures CTs vision from truck and highway, very effective for taking the A bombsite
Cons- CTs can use it to sneak into the site
D: Truck
Pros- Obscures CTs vision from truck
Cons- Minimal Problems
E: Highway
Pros- Obscures CTs vision from highway into the A bomb site
Cons- Minimal Problems
F: Whitebox
Pros- Obscures CTs vision down highway and towards mid depending on position
Cons- Minimal Problems
G: "Z"
Pros- Obscures CTs vision over mid from the Z hallway, great for dealing with AWPers
Cons- Minimal Problems
H: Sandbags
Pros- Obscures CTs vision over mid from the sandbags, great for dealing with AWPers
Cons- Minimal Problems
I: Heaven
Pros- Obscures CTs vision over the B bomb site from heaven
Cons- Minimal Problems
J: B Main
Pros- Obscures CTs vision into B main and allows travel into checkers undetected
Cons- Obscures your teams vision into the bombsite
K: Tree Room
Pros- Obscures CTs vision into the B bomb site from the tree room
Cons- Minimal Problems
