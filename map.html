Map Callouts



Labeled Map of Cache

TREE ROOM- Tree for short, called tree room as it has a tree in it
HEAVEN– The ramp and platform overlooking the B bombsite
HEADSHOT– The lower area next to the bomb site
CHECKERS– The area by the B bomb site that has a checkered tile flooring
B MAIN– The main entrance to the B bomb site for Terrorist
FLASH WINDOW– A window you can break and throw grenades through to the B bombsite
CONNECTOR- Room between garage and the hallways from T spawn to B main
GARAGE- The terrorist side of mid, a building that is a garage
BOOST- Boxes the terrorist often boost onto snipe from
A LONG– The long section between garage and squeaky
SQUEAKY– A section of rooms that lead to a blue door that squeaks when it opens
A MAIN– Main entrance for terrorist into the A bomb site
FORKLIFT- A forklift and some boxes CT often hide behind
CATWALK– An elevated section that looks over the A bombsite
HIGHWAY- Section of the map that connects mid to the A bombsite
WHITE BOX- A box with a white tarp of it, sometimes called suger cube
SANDBAGS– A pile of sandbags in the mid area
MID- middle of the map, large open area with lots of routes
MID ROOF– the roof of a building in mid
VENTS– Air Vents that connect mid to checkers
"Z"– A "Z" shaped hallway that connects mid to CT spawn
TRUCK- A parked truck, usually entails the entire corner of that part of the map
ELECTRIC BOX- An eletrical box that players can jump on top of
