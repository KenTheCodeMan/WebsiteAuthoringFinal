TERMINOLOGY



To Save
To save is to try not to die in the current round to carry over your weapons to the next round.
Eco Round
Eco, short for economy, is a term used to describe a round where little to no spending to ensure you have enough money for a good buy next round.
lit / tagged
Someone who is lit or tagged has received dammage but not died.
Ace
An ace is when a single player gets the final hit to kill all five players of the enemy team.
GH
Shorthand for good half.
NS
Shorthand for nice shot.
NT
Shorthand for nice try.
Wall Bang
To shoot someone through a wall with a weapon that can penetrate walls.
Stack
Stack can have multiple meanings, one is to put all of a teams players in one place, or for one player to stand on top of the other to gain vision over a wall.
To Boost
Boosting entails a player crouching to let another player stand on his head to reach a spot he could not normally get to by himself.
Need Drop
A player can ask for a drop, which means when he does not have enough money to buy himself a good weapon, and one of his team mates can give him one.
Ninja Defuse
To lay down a smoke grenade on the bomb and defuse the bomb. The terrorist vision is obscured by the smoke and makes it more difficult to stop the defuser.
Smurf
A player who's main account is ranked higher then the one they are currently playing on.
GLHF
Shorthand for good luck, have fun.
GG
Shorthand for good game.
Legged
Generally refers to an individual who was shot in the leg by an AWP, as the dammage received from a leg shot is not enough to kill a player.
Kappa
A Twitch.tv emote that is used to show sarcasm.
Buy Out
To spend all of your money as it is the last round of the game or last game of the half.
Pick, Get a Pick
To kill a player in the beginning part of the match.
Prefire
To start firing a weapon before a player sees another player, generally when going around corners where they expect someone to be.
CT
Shorthand for counter-terrorist.
T
Shorthand for Terrorist.
CT/T Spawn
The starting area for T or CT.
Rotate
For players to move from the location they are currently in to where the team may need them.
BM
Shorthand for bad mannered.
Frag
To kill or eliminate an enemy player.
Strat
Shorthand for strategy. A strategy is a method or plan of attack.
Rush
To rush is to run into an area with no hesitation.
Walk
To walk within the game, when walking you generate no noise from footsteps.
