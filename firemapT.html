Terrorist Fires




A: Quad, Back Corner of Site
Pros- Removes CTs from corner of the site
Cons- Minimal problems
B: Wall Against Squeaky
Pros- Removes CTs from that corner
Cons- Minimal problems
C: Forklift
Pros- Removes CTs from standing around forklift
Cons- Minimal problems
D: Truck
Pros- Stops CTs from rotating through the truck area
Cons- Your team will unable to flank highway until it clears
E: White Box
Pros- Removes CTs from behind the box
Cons- Can block travel down highway to the A bomb site
F: "Z"
Pros- Stops CTs going through the hallway
Cons- Blocks travel into CT spawn from mid
G: Sandbags
Pros- Great for flushing CTs who are hiding there into the open for an easy kill
Cons- Minimal problems
H: Ninja Box
Pros- Great for flushing CTs who are hiding behind the box for an easy kill
Cons- Minimal problems
I: Vents
Pros- Keeps CTs from coming through vents and traveling through the doorway
Cons- Also limits your teams travel through the area
J: B Main Box
Pros- Flushes out CTs hiding behind the box for an easy kill
Cons- This fire can block travel through the door to checkers
K: Heaven
Pros- Removes CTs from heaven
Cons- Minimal problems
L: Tree Room
Pros- Stops travel from tree room to the bomb site
Cons- Keeps your team within the bomb site
M: Headshot
Pros- Flushes CTs out of the headshot area
Cons- Minimal problems
N: Back of Site
Pros- Flushes CTs out of the back of the site
Cons- Can prevent your team from planting the bomb there
